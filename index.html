ABOUT US

15 years of crafting one-of-a-kind pieces

We don't mass-produce furniture. Every piece is a unique project, born from a dialogue with the client and deep respect for the material.

Our artisans work exclusively with premium hardwood: oak, walnut, ash, and beech. Every board undergoes rigorous selection and extended kiln drying.
